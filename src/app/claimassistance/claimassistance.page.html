GET IMMEDIATE CLAIM ASSISTANCE

Processing motor insurance claim is easier than you think. It’s much simpler using the following steps to settle claims for an accident

-LONE ACCIDENT

Completed claim form. (This can be optional if the mobile app is developed with the claim form interface)
Estimate of repairs on a garage letter head.
Photographs explicitly showing the registration number and damaged area of the vehicle.
Driving license of the driver

- WHEN THIRD-PARTY IS INVOLVED.

Certificate of Insurance of the Negligent third-party.
Photocopy of the Negligent third-party Driver’s licence.
Undertaking letter from the Negligent third-party admitting liability.
All require documents for the lone accident

- VANDALIZED VEHICLE

Completed vandalized completed claim form.
Estimate of repairs on garage letter head.
Photographs explicitly showing the registration number and damaged area of the vehicle.
Driving license of the driver.
Police report
